    Live data auto-refresh (optional)

    By default the app refreshes scores & goalscorers on every reload from the free open-data project openfootball/worldcup.json (GitHub, public domain — no key, updated about once a day). For true live, minute-by-minute scores, switch to API-Football — note their Free plan does not cover the current season, so World Cup 2026 live data requires one of their paid plans. The daily 08:00 update keeps everything (cards, lineups, stats) in sync regardless.
